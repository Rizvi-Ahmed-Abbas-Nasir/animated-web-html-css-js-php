About us

About us Our Promise

Hapros Global is dedicated to bridging the trust gap in the global supply chain. We understand sourcing the right products, quality, and price, and we aim to make the process smooth and transparent.

With our deep industry knowledge, extensive network, and unwavering dedication to quality, we extend your procurement team’s reach, ensuring precise and caring fulfillment of your needs.

Choose Hapros Global for over a century of experience, trust, and excellence. We handle sourcing and logistics, so you can focus on growing your business confidently.

Who are we?

Hapros Global, an international sourcing company, has over 125 years of experience sourcing high-quality food grains, pulses, spices, oilseeds, and other conventional food ingredients.

Hapros Global, a fifth-generation business founded in 1899, has endured through history, from pre-independence India to the modern era.

Our resilient journey is built on our business ethos: trust, quality, and enduring strong relationships spanning over 125 years.

Our reliable delivery ensures timely receipt of products, meeting quality standards. Our extensive network and robust supply chain provide customized solutions and reliable service across India and globally.

We lead the food industry by predicting market trends and managing supply chains efficiently.
