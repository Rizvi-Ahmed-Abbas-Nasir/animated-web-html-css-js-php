About Us

Our Legacy

Since 1899, Hapros Global has been at the forefront of the agricultural industry, delivering high-quality food ingredients worldwide. Our journey started with Ganpat Pasu, a visionary from Gujarat, and over five generations, we have expanded into sourcing, processing, trading, and exporting to over 20 countries.

About Us

Our Story

"Hapros Global, founded in 1899 by Ganpat Pasu, has built strong partnerships with farmers over five generations to promote fair trade and sustainable practices. As we expand globally, our commitment to quality and ethical sourcing remains steadfast. We continue this legacy with a focus on a brighter, sustainable future for generations to come."

Our Mission

Empowering Farmer Communities

Hapros Global is committed to a sustainable future for food production. Our journey began in 1899 with Ganpat Pasu's vision, and over five generations, we have cultivated partnerships with farmers, embraced sustainable practices, and expanded our reach to over 20 countries.

At the heart of our success are the farmers. Through close partnerships, fair trade practices, and educational programs, we strive to uplift and empower agricultural communities, ensuring a brighter, more sustainable future for all.
